Article 2:

"Solar Glass Could Convert The Windows Of Every Building Into Power-Generating Panels"
By: Scott Snowden

Photovoltaic glass is an up and coming technology in the world of construction and civil engineering. This glass takes light from the sun and converts it into energy. It harvests light that is not visable to the human eye, so the glass appears normal and see through.
